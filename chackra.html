Chakra Breathing Meditation

Chakra Breathing Meditation is a practice that brings awareness and energy to each of the seven chakras. This active meditation uses rapid breathing and gentle movement to revitalize the chakras, accompanied by music for a guided experience. Follow the steps below to explore each chakra.

First Stage: Breathing Up Through Each Chakra (45 minutes)

  • Stand comfortably: Position your feet shoulder-width apart, keeping your body relaxed.
  • Focus on the first chakra: Begin deep, rapid breathing directed to the pelvic area (first chakra) with an open mouth.
  • Move up with each bell: Shift your breathing to the next chakra with each bell, making each upward stage gentler and faster.
  • Maintain natural movement: Allow slight body movements, like shaking or stretching, but keep your feet grounded.
  • Stay relaxed: Keep your joints loose, letting your awareness settle into the sensations of each chakra.
  • After reaching the seventh chakra: Gradually let your breathing ease back down through each chakra.

Second Stage: Rest and Silence (15 minutes)

After the breathing cycles, sit or lie down with your eyes closed. Enter a state of calm and observation, witnessing sensations within without judgment or focus. This stage allows you to integrate the energy and awareness built during the breathing.
